Part 1 — The Why

The Goal

Get out of the hump before your early-September event — not by white-knuckling a crash diet, but by rebuilding a rhythm you already know works for you. Body Pump and spin got you into the best shape of your life once. This block puts them back to work, structured around your actual numbers instead of guesswork.

A 3D body scan around the same period read 35.7% body fat instead of 42% — that's a method difference, not a real change. DEXA is the tracking baseline for this block; the two body-fat percentages are never compared against each other.

The Numbers

Daily target: ~1,800 kcal, protein ~120g/day — roughly a palm-sized source at each meal plus a snack. That's a ~400 kcal deficit off a Moderately Active TDEE of 2,195 kcal — desk job, 3 structured strength/cardio sessions/week (Tue/Thu strength + spin) plus daily walking, matching what's actually confirmed below. At ~1 lb every 8-9 days, that's roughly 7-8 lbs by early September, staying well clear of your 1,416 RMR floor.

Tracking: log loosely weeks 1-2 to calibrate, then plate-method + one weekly weigh-in. Waist check every 2 weeks.

Milestone Rewards

CheckpointSuggested reward
Wk 3Something small and immediate - a new workout playlist, a cute gym accessory, or a solo night off to watch whatever you want.
Wk 5-6A bit bigger, since this is where body comp changes usually start showing - a massage, a new workout outfit, or new sneakers.
Wk 9 · EventThe big one - an outfit for the event itself, or an experience (not food-based) that marks finishing what you started. Also: get the rescan. Seeing the number move is its own reward.

These are starting suggestions - swap in whatever actually motivates you. The only rule: non-food, and something you wouldn't otherwise just buy yourself.

The One Rule: Never Miss Twice

Missing a single session is noise - it happens, it doesn't matter. Missing two in a row is how a first attempt quietly falls apart. If you skip a day, the only job the next day has is to not be skipped too. Everything else on this page is flexible. This rule isn't.

How This Block Progresses

PhaseStays the sameWhat changes
Wk 1-2
Foundation		 Full weekly structure as laid out above Tue/Thu: rotate through the three 60-min options. Just building the habit. Loose food logging to calibrate 1,800 kcal.
Wk 3-4
First Read		 Same schedule Check the weight trend at wk 3. Flat or up → tighten by 150-200 kcal. On track → hold. Keep rotating Tue/Thu options.
Wk 5-6
Load Up		 Same days, same structure Tue/Thu: increase dumbbell weight within these sessions. Mon: move to Opt 2, 25 Min Lower Body. Re-check RHR and waist.
Wk 7-8
Sharpen		 Same schedule Continue the 60-min rotation, heavier weight. If progress has stalled, tighten calories OR add load - not both at once.
Wk 9
Event Week		 Keep Tue/Thu strength + spin as normal early in the week Taper: switch Tue/Thu to the 30 Min Tempo standalone - shorter, lighter. Prioritize sleep and hydration. No new intensity this week.
